Superposition’s EmpowHERment Conference aims to inspire women of all backgrounds to become proactive leaders in today’s society through our series of workshops, panels, and scholarship opportunities.

We hope to spread insight from women leaders in STEAM, business, activism, and more to young females around the globe.

Opportunities

Scholarships

APPLY

We will be offering scholarships to support women in Social Impact & Education. Each category will have 3 prizes: $250, $150, $100. Application information will be released soon. Applicants may apply to more than one category. We’re also offering an exclusive $100 scholarship available only to Superposition ambassadors of any gender identity.

Become an Ambassador

APPLY

Being an EmpowHERment Conference ambassador means that you’ll be making a difference in someone’s life by bringing them into this empowering experience. You can help bridge the gender gap in STEM by motivating more girls and providing them with inspiring role models they can hear from at the EmpowHERment Conference! With your help, we can bring STEAM opportunities to more communities across the world.

What You'll Get

  • Gain valuable experience as a brand ambassador
  • Opportunities to foster new connections with other ambassadors
  • Raffles & giveaways
  • Eligibility to get hours for the Presidential Volunteer Service Award
  • An ambassador-only scholarship opportunity

Frequently Asked Questions


Attending the event is completely free!

Our EmpowHERment Conference is open to everyone, regardless of age and gender identity.

The conference will be 100% online!

The event is scheduled to be from 10 AM PST - 4 PM PST on January 16th, 2021. Check out the schedule above for more details about specific workshops!

While it’s recommended to attend most of the conference, we do not require attending all workshops.

Our Social Impact and Education Scholarships are available to female-identifying and non-binary individuals. However, our Education Scholarship is open only to students enrolled in any educational institution. The Ambassador Scholarship is available only to Superposition ambassadors.
